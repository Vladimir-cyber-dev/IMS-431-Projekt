Frequently Asked Questions

  • What can I do if the app does not start?

    Ensure your device meets the system requirements and that you have the latest version of the app installed. If the problem persists, contact our support.

  • How can I restore my saved projects?

    Check the location of your projects and ensure they have not been accidentally deleted. If you have a backup, you can restore it or contact our support for further assistance.

  • The app keeps crashing. What should I do?

    Try restarting your device and reinstalling the app. Ensure your operating system is up to date. If issues persist, please reach out to our support team for help.

  • How can I retrieve my license or registration key again?

    Log in to your user account on our website to retrieve your license key. If you do not have access, contact our support for assistance.
